About the course

An 8‑week, live cohort on no‑code AI tools for work and research. Curriculum covers practical GenAI for literature & text analysis, data workflows, automation, custom GPTs, and research communication.

Modules
  1. Websites, documents & presentations — LLM basics; generating Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; building simple research sites; AI for scientific presentations.
  2. AI‑automated data analysis — Code Interpreter, dashboards, batch file processing, automated visualizations and reports.
  3. GenAI for research & text analysis — Literature reviews; text classification; sentiment; knowledge maps; summarization pipelines.
  4. Custom GPTs and chatbots — Context windows; RAG; Botpress; integrating knowledge bases.
  5. AI for task automation — Email automations; agent‑based flows; browser control agents.
  6. GenAI for communication & outreach — Figures and diagrams; video abstracts; visualization prompts; content scheduling.
  7. Capstone project & showcase — Progress checks; peer feedback; presentations; public Q&A.
  8. Assessment & reflection — Wrap‑up and portfolio polish.
